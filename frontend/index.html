🧘 5-Min Mindfulness
5:00
Tap to Start
1

Find Your Position

Sit comfortably with your back straight. Close your eyes gently. Place your hands on your lap.

2

Focus on Breath

Notice the natural rhythm of your breathing. Don't try to control it — just observe the rise and fall.

3

Notice Sensations

Observe thoughts without judging them. When your mind wanders, gently bring it back to your breath.

4

Body Scan

Slowly scan from head to toe. Notice any tension and consciously relax each area as you go.

5

Return Gently

When the timer ends, slowly open your eyes. Take a moment before resuming your day.

💪 Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense each muscle group for 5 seconds, then release for 30 seconds. Feel the difference.
